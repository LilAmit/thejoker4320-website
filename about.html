About The Joker

Founded in 2012, a FIRST Israel District team built on curiosity, teamwork and a little bit of wild.

The Joker #4320 is a FIRST® Robotics Competition team from Petah Tikva, Israel, part of the AMAL network and the FIRST Israel District. Every season we design, build and program a competition robot in just a few intense weeks, but the robot is only half the story.

We're a place where students learn engineering, code, design, business and leadership, and where they inspire the next generation to fall in love with STEAM. Whatever hand the season deals us, we play it together, and that's why we call ourselves your winning card.
